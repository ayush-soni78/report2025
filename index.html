Pre‑Placement Training Program Report 2025

Footwear Design & Development Institute (FDDI), Chhindwara — joint campus initiative with FDDI Guna

On Campus Program held at FDDI Chhindwara campus
Dates: 22–29 December 2025
Total participants: 88 final‑year students (Chhindwara + Guna)

Participating schools (final‑year students)

School of Retail & Fashion Merchandise — MBA students (31+7)
School of Fashion Designing — Bachelor of Fashion Design students (30)
School of Footwear Design & Production — Bachelor of Footwear Design & Production students (12+8)
Mentor: Dr. Atanu Mandal Outcome: Students empowered with industry‑ready skills and confidence

Program overview

A special one‑week program with mock GD, practice workshops, mock interviews/PI, and industry sessions to help students improve their job skills and get ready for placements.

GD/PI, Aptitude, Sector Orientation
